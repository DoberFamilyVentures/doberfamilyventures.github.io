# Dober Family Ventures — Codex Context

This folder gives Codex project context for working on the Dober Family Ventures website and related brand assets.

Dober Family Ventures is a family-owned operating company / family office brand. It exists to build, operate, and support practical businesses that strengthen households. The site should feel calm, durable, trustworthy, and long-term oriented — not flashy, startup-like, or venture-capital aggressive.

## Core positioning

**Primary idea:** Building businesses that strengthen households.

**Brand role:** Parent company / holding company for practical household-focused ventures.

**Current ventures:**

- **Maintley** — current operating business focused on household operations, maintenance continuity, asset history, recurring care, and home organization.
- **Family Economy** — in development; a family system for teaching responsibility, earning, saving, decision-making, and financial literacy in a safe household environment.
- **Future Holdings** — future ventures that fit the same values and long-term household-strengthening mission.

## Site direction

The website should communicate:

1. Who Dober Family Ventures is.
2. Why the ventures belong together.
3. What the current ventures are.
4. How the company thinks long term.
5. How visitors can navigate to Maintley and Family Economy.

The page should avoid sounding like a private equity fund, acquisition shop, or traditional venture capital firm. It should sound like a family-owned operating company building and stewarding practical businesses.
