# Dober Family Ventures — Website Structure

## Recommended homepage flow

The homepage should move through a clear visitor journey:

1. **Hero** — identify the company and mission.
2. **Focus / Beliefs** — explain what kinds of businesses belong here.
3. **Current Ventures** — show what has actually been built or is being developed.
4. **Approach / Principles** — explain how the company operates and thinks long-term.
5. **Footer** — provide navigation to ventures and contact/about links.

## Hero section

Purpose: communicate the brand in under 10 seconds.

Recommended elements:

- Small eyebrow: `FAMILY OFFICE | OPERATING COMPANY`
- Secondary eyebrow: `LONG-TERM STEWARDSHIP`
- Main heading: `Dober Family Ventures`
- Mission headline: `Building businesses that strengthen households.`
- Supporting paragraph explaining practical businesses, families, and long-term value.
- CTAs:
  - `Explore holdings`
  - `Our approach`

## Hero portfolio card

Purpose: visually explain that Maintley, Family Economy, and Future Holdings belong under the same parent company.

Recommended structure:

- Card label: `PORTFOLIO FAMILY`
- Heading: `Businesses that belong to the same long view.`
- Top node: `DOBER FAMILY VENTURES` or `OPERATING PORTFOLIO`
- Featured card: `MAINTLEY` with status `Current operating business`
- Smaller cards:
  - `FAMILY ECONOMY` with status `In development`
  - `FUTURE HOLDINGS` with status `Future ventures`

Maintley should be visually dominant because it is the current operating business.

## What we focus on

Purpose: explain the categories or beliefs connecting the ventures.

Recommended cards:

- `HOME SERVICES` — Practical businesses with recurring demand.
- `FAMILY SUPPORT` — Ideas that improve how households function.
- `PATIENT CAPITAL` — Built to hold, not flip.

This section can stay more principle-based than product-based.

## Current Ventures section

Purpose: provide clear navigation to other product/app pages.

Recommended placement: after `What we focus on` and before deeper principles.

Recommended cards:

### Maintley
Current operating business. Household maintenance, asset history, recurring care, and operational memory for the home.

CTA: `Visit Maintley` or `Explore Maintley`

### Family Economy
In development. A household system for teaching responsibility, earning, saving, and decision-making.

CTA: `Learn about Family Economy`

### Future Holdings
Future ventures that fit the same household-strengthening mission.

CTA can be omitted or replaced with `Coming later`.

## Approach / Principles section

The current process-style section may feel too venture-capital-like if it uses language such as `Search`, `Support`, `Strengthen`, `Hold`.

Preferred alternative section title:

- `What Guides Us`
- `How We Build`
- `Operating Principles`

Preferred principles:

1. **Practical First** — We focus on useful businesses solving real household problems.
2. **Systems Matter** — Strong systems create stronger homes and stronger companies.
3. **Families Matter** — The best businesses improve life at home.
4. **Long-Term Thinking** — We build for decades, not quarters.

## Footer

Footer should be simple and useful.

Recommended links:

- Maintley
- Family Economy
- Our Approach
- Contact

Footer copy:

`Dober Family Ventures — Building businesses that strengthen households.`
