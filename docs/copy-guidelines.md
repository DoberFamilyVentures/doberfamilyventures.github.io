# Dober Family Ventures — Copy Guidelines

## Preferred tone

Write with calm confidence. The voice should feel mature, useful, and grounded.

The site should sound like a family-owned operating company with a long-term view — not like a startup pitch deck, private equity fund, or venture capital landing page.

## Good headline patterns

- Building businesses that strengthen households.
- Practical businesses. Stronger households. Long-term stewardship.
- Useful ventures for homes, families, and everyday life.
- Built to serve households over the long view.

## Good section titles

- What we focus on
- Current ventures
- What guides us
- How we build
- Operating principles
- Built to hold, not flip
- Businesses that belong to the same long view

## Wording to prefer

Use:

- build
- operate
- support
- steward
- strengthen
- improve
- practical
- useful
- household
- family
- home
- systems
- consistency
- recurring care
- long-term
- durable
- operating business
- in development
- future ventures

## Wording to avoid or limit

Avoid sounding like an acquisition fund or VC firm. Limit or replace:

- deal flow
- acquire
- exits
- portfolio optimization
- operational edge
- investment thesis
- alpha
- unlock value
- dry powder
- aggressive growth
- disruption
- scale at all costs
- capital deployment
- roll-up strategy

## Replace this wording

Instead of:

`Search — We look for businesses with clear value and a real operational edge.`

Use:

`Practical First — We focus on useful businesses solving real household problems.`

Instead of:

`Support — We bring capital, structure, and an owner mindset to the table.`

Use:

`Systems Matter — We bring structure, consistency, and care to the businesses we build.`

Instead of:

`Strengthen — We aim to leave every company more durable than we found it.`

Use:

`Families Matter — The best businesses leave households better than they found them.`

Instead of:

`Hold — Long-term compounding matters more than short-term noise.`

Use:

`Long-Term Thinking — We build for decades, not quarters.`

## Maintley copy direction

Maintley should be described as a current operating business focused on household operations.

Good phrases:

- Household maintenance and operational memory.
- A calmer way to manage home maintenance, assets, and recurring care.
- Helping homeowners keep track of what was done, what is due, and what matters next.

Avoid framing Maintley as a basic task reminder app. It is more about maintenance continuity, home history, and household operations.

## Family Economy copy direction

Family Economy should be described as in development.

Good phrases:

- A household system for teaching responsibility and financial decision-making.
- A safe family environment for earning, saving, planning, and learning.
- Helping children practice money decisions before real-world consequences arrive.

Avoid making it sound like only a chores and allowance app. Chores may be part of the system, but the bigger story is household financial literacy and responsibility.
