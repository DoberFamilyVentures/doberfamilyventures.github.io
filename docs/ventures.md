# Dober Family Ventures — Venture Context

## Parent company

Dober Family Ventures is the parent brand. It connects several ventures under one mission:

**Build businesses that strengthen households.**

The parent site should explain why the ventures belong together and route users to the correct product pages.

## Maintley

Status: Current operating business.

Category: Home services / household operations.

Maintley helps homeowners manage the operational side of homeownership. It should not be framed as just a to-do list or reminder app.

Core themes:

- Maintenance continuity
- Household operational memory
- Home asset tracking
- Recurring care
- System history
- Calm homeowner organization
- Reducing forgotten maintenance
- Keeping the home standard high over time

Simple description:

`Maintley helps homeowners keep track of maintenance, assets, recurring care, and the history of their home.`

CTA language:

- Visit Maintley
- Explore Maintley
- View Maintley

## Family Economy

Status: In development.

Category: Family support / household financial literacy.

Family Economy is a family system that helps children practice responsibility, earning, saving, planning, and decision-making in a safe household environment.

Core themes:

- Mock family economy
- Financial literacy
- Responsibility
- Earning and saving
- Family systems
- Safe practice environment
- Rewards and decision-making
- Parent-guided learning

Do not reduce it to only chores and allowance. Chores may exist, but the broader concept is a household learning economy.

Simple description:

`Family Economy is a household system for teaching responsibility, earning, saving, and financial decision-making in a safe family environment.`

CTA language:

- Learn about Family Economy
- Explore Family Economy
- Coming soon

## Future Holdings

Status: Future ventures.

Future Holdings represents future businesses or projects that fit the same household-strengthening mission.

It should not imply that many companies already exist. Keep it honest and forward-looking.

Good description:

`Future ventures that fit the same values: practical, durable, useful, and household-centered.`

## Relationship between ventures

The site should make clear that Maintley and Family Economy are not random separate projects.

They both fit the same thesis:

- Maintley strengthens the physical and operational side of the home.
- Family Economy strengthens the educational and financial systems inside the home.
- Future ventures should strengthen households in similarly practical ways.

## Navigation recommendations

Visitors should be able to reach Maintley and Family Economy from:

1. Header navigation, if included.
2. Hero portfolio card, if clickable.
3. Dedicated Current Ventures section.
4. Footer.

Do not rely only on the hero card. A dedicated Current Ventures section is the clearest path for users.
